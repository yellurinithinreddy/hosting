Dos & Don'ts in Group Discussion

Dos Don'ts
Be well-prepared and knowledgeable about the topic. Interrupt others while they are speaking.
Listen actively to other participants. Dominate the discussion.
Speak clearly and confidently. Get aggressive or confrontational.
Respect others' opinions and viewpoints. Stray off-topic or digress.
Maintain eye contact with the group. Use inappropriate or offensive language.
Use positive body language. Ignore or dismiss others' contributions.
Keep your points concise and relevant. Speak too fast or too slow.
Encourage participation from quieter members. Overuse gestures or body movements.
Summarize your points effectively. Be overly critical or negative.
